Frequently asked questions

Can I contribute an article?

Yes — we accept guest contributions from practitioners in sales, marketing, and CRM. Send a short pitch to our pitches email and we'll get back to you within a few days.

Do you offer advertising or sponsorships?

We work with a limited number of sponsors each quarter. Reach out through the contact form above and select "Partnership or advertising" so it reaches the right team.

How quickly will I hear back?

We aim to respond to every message within one business day. Story pitches may take a little longer since they go through editorial review.

Can I request a correction on an article?

Absolutely. Email us with the article title and the correction, and our editorial team will review and update it as needed.
