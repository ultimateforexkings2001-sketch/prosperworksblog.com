What Is CRM? A Beginner's Guide

Published: July 2026

What is CRM?

Customer Relationship Management (CRM) is a strategy and type of software that helps businesses organize customer information, manage sales opportunities, and improve communication with clients.

Why is CRM important?

A CRM system allows businesses to keep track of customer interactions, automate routine tasks, and provide better customer service. It also helps sales teams stay organized and identify new opportunities.

Common CRM Features

  • Contact management
  • Lead tracking
  • Sales pipeline management
  • Email integration
  • Reporting and analytics
  • Task reminders

Final Thoughts

Whether you're a freelancer, startup, or large company, a CRM can help you build stronger customer relationships and improve your sales process.
